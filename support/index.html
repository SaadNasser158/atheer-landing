Support

How can we help?

Find answers to common questions below, or reach out directly — we're quick to respond.

How do I add a podcast?

Tap the Discover tab to search and browse podcasts. Tap any show and hit Subscribe — it'll appear in your Library instantly.

How do transcripts work?

Atheer fetches transcripts from the podcast's RSS feed when available. While an episode is playing, tap the transcript icon in the player to follow along word-by-word.

Is Atheer free?

Yes — Atheer is completely free to download and use. No account required, no subscription needed.

Which languages are supported?

The Discover feed supports English, Arabic (العربية), Spanish, and French. Switch between them any time from the Discover tab.

My episodes aren't loading. What should I do?

Check your internet connection first. If the issue persists, pull down to refresh the feed. For persistent issues, try removing and re-adding the show from your Library.

How do I control playback speed?

In the full player, tap the speed indicator (e.g. 1×) to cycle through speeds from 0.5× up to 3×.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. Atheer is local-first — your library, playback history, and preferences stay on your device. Nothing is synced to a server.
