What it does

Built around the episode, not around the app.

Every screen is designed to keep you inside the listening experience. No feed anxiety, no badge pressure — just the podcast, when you want it.

Live transcripts

Words scroll with the playhead. Tap any line to jump there, bookmark moments, or search the full episode without stopping.

Clip export

Pull a 15–60 second segment, frame it with optional captions, and share it directly from the player. No editing app required.

Multilingual discovery

Switch between English, Arabic, Spanish, and French with one tap. Trending charts and new shows update per language.

Local-first library

Your subscriptions, queue, and progress live on-device. No account required for core use — open the app and press play.

AI chapters

Episodes without embedded chapters get them generated automatically from the transcript — so every long-form show is navigable.

Ambient player

The full-screen player samples the artwork's dominant color to build an immersive backdrop that changes with every episode.

The thinking behind it

Podcast apps got complicated. Atheer didn't.

01

One calm layout

Rounded typography, monochrome surfaces, and artwork that drives the color. No dashboard, no noise.

02

Continuous flow

Artwork, metadata, transcript, and clip creation feel like adjacent states — not separate screens bolted together.

03

Immediate access

No onboarding gates for listening. Open the app, find a show, press play. Sign-in is optional.

04

Speed that respects you

Artwork loads fast, playback is gapless, downloads work in the background. The app moves at your pace.

Full capability list
